ABOUT ME

My name is Shawn Kost, and I was born and raised in Irvine, California. Growing up I was involved in multiple different sports and activities, but my two favorite things were baseball and video games. My love for video games ultimately led me to a love for computers and technology. After highschool, I got a job as a computer technician where I learned how to integrate different types of computer systems and servers. I just recently got into web development, and i'm excited to learn more and expand my portfolio.
